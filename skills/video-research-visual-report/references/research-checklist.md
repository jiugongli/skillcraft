# Research Checklist

Use this checklist when the video makes claims that need verification.

- Video transcript or audio transcription collected
- Video description and cited links reviewed
- Original article/report/paper/product page found
- Official source checked
- Third-party analysis checked
- Community/user feedback checked
- Opposing or skeptical view checked
- Key numbers verified where possible
- Source incentives considered
- Benchmark/test methodology considered
- Facts separated from interpretation
- Uncertainty stated explicitly
